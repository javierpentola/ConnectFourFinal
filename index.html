Rules

Objective

Be the first player to connect 4 discs of the same color in a row (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally).

How to Play

  • Red goes first in the first game.
  • Players must alternate turns, and only one disc can be dropped per turn.
  • The game ends when there is a 4 in a row or a stalemate.
  • The player who started the previous game goes second in the next game.
